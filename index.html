About Us

Migration Advisory Centre (MAC)

The Migration Advisory Centre (MAC) is a division of Right Fit Evidence Pty Ltd, a multidisciplinary professional services firm based in Maseru, Lesotho. MAC was established to provide trusted, transparent migration advisory services to individuals and businesses navigating immigration requirements in Lesotho and abroad.

We believe every foreign national deserves clarity and dignity in their migration journey. That's why we go beyond paperwork — we ensure our clients are informed, prepared, and supported at every step.

Vision

A Lesotho where all foreign nationals lawfully reside and work with dignity, clarity, and ease.

Mission

To contribute to ethical, evidence-informed migration by equipping clients with professional guidance and transparent processes.

What Happens Next

1️⃣
We review your submission Our advisor assesses your eligibility based on the information you provide — typically within one business day.
