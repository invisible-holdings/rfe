About Us

Right Fit Evidence Pty Ltd

Right Fit Evidence (RFE) Pty Ltd is a multidisciplinary professional services firm based in Maseru, Lesotho. We were established to provide evidence-based research, migration advisory, web development, due diligence, and consulting services to individuals, businesses, governments and NGOs — locally and across borders.

We believe the right evidence, in the right hands, at the right time, can transform outcomes. That's why we go beyond data collection — we ensure our outputs are relevant, timely, and embedded in decisions that matter.

Vision

A Lesotho where all foreign nationals lawfully reside and work with dignity, clarity, and ease — and where evidence drives every important decision.

Mission

To contribute to ethical, evidence-informed progress by equipping clients with professional guidance, rigorous research, and technology-driven solutions.
